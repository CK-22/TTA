BTS

BTS an acronym for Bangtan Sonyeondan, which translates to bulletproof boys or 'Beyond the Scene' is a South Korean group, they have been recognised for their hard work, authenticity and self-produced music since their debut in 2013. This Grammy-nominated group, although they have been acknowledged and have captured the hearts of many, experienced many trials and tribulations on their path to their popularity.
