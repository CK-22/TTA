BTS

They are a Korean boy group, that consists of 7 members that debuted in 2013, under Bighit Entertainment, BTS, an acronym for‘Beyond the Scene’, but was originally stood for Bangtan Sonyeondan, which translates to bulletproof boys. This Grammy-nominated group have had their talents acknowledged, and captured the hearts of many, but the journey to this popularity and fame was no easy road for them.

BTS and their Achievements

This group has achieved many great things, but they stand as one of the only Kpop acts that has opened and paved the way for ther Kpop acts to be recognised and awarded in the westen music industry.

-The group is well-known for cowriting and coproducing their own music.
-They have earned 7 nominations for the 2022 Billboard Music nominations
-They were nominated for 4 consecutive years for the Top Duo/group award which they won, in 2019 and 2021
-They are also the first Kpop Group to address the United Nations
