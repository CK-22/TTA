Guide to Koh Samui

The third largest Island in Thailand, Koh Samui is small enough to be able to travel around Island with no
hassel on by motorbike or car in just a couple of hours.
The Island has a variety of beaches and activities to do during your stay, you could visit the beutiful beaches
like Lami, Chaweng, Taling and Natien. In addition to the beaches and plentiful nature, Koh Samui is full of
history and culture.
