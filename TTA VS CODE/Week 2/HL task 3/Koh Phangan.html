Guide to Koh Phangan

Koh Phangan is a large is land off the east coast of Surat Thani province in the Gulf of Thailand, it is located about
100km offshore and about 15km north of Koh Samui, Thailands leading Tourist Attraction.
Most well-known for its monthly full moon Party, Koh Phangan features some of the most spectacular Beaches.
Apart from the beautiful beaches and deep blue sea water, the land offers tourists natural attractions like forests,
waterfalls, temples and you can take a ferry to Tao island to sight see coral reef and aquatic animals.
The land hosts culteral and adventurous sites like Tan Sadet National Waterfall and many more!
